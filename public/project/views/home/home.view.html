What to Wear, What to Wear...

Do you consider yourself a fashionista? Is your go to outfit sweats and an old T-shirt? Whatever your style, you can make the best of your closet with What to Wear. What to Wear uses the weather reports for your area and real information about your closet to pick out outfits for you. No more rifling through your drawers and wrinkling everything looking for something that isn't even clean. No more trying on outfit after outfit only to walk outside in your cute sundress to a torrential downpour. You can now just scroll through your options on your computer, select an outfit, mark it as dirty, and put it on!
